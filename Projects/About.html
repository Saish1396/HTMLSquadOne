Projects

  • Yiblr Alarm App
    I started the idea of this alarm clock back in late 2014. I absolutely hated having to swipe to turn off an alarm and having to face the challenge of a bright screen against your tearing eyes, day after day. So, in Winter 2015 (at CodeDay NJ), I collaborated with Peter Soboyejo and Haris Jagannathan to create the alarm clock that listens.
    It uses the:

    1. Voice detection software in your device to respond to your morning shouting.
    2. Gyroscope in your device to respond to shifts in your device, such as flipping your device over
    3. Proximity Sensor in your device to respond to hand waves or swipes over your device

    This app will be available on the Google Play Store soon, and for more information, check out our Yiblr Facebook Page.
